# Capability model

Flexsimulation models value through capabilities, not only through assets or systems.

## Capability types

```text
System capabilities: shared capabilities that create system effects across assets and customers.
Edge capabilities: asset-specific or use-case-specific capabilities connected to the system core.
```

## Initial system capabilities

- Customer and asset information model
- Observable energy state
- 15-minute plan and baseline management
- Stacked optimisation
- Flexibility forecasting and risk quantification
- Portfolio virtual battery management
- Activation orchestration
- Rebound scheduling and intraday monetisation
- Delivery verification and settlement
- Business case and value attribution
- Cross-asset portfolio effects and dependency modelling

## Initial edge capabilities

- EV smart charging to flexibility markets
- EV V2X to flexibility markets
- Cabin battery flexibility
- PV forecast and curtailment
- Heat pump and hot-water flexibility
- Cabin heating and frost-protection flexibility
- Pool or premium-cabin load flexibility
- Dual-home energy sharing
- Home battery optimisation

## Strategic principle

Edge capabilities are optional and can be sequenced. System capabilities are multipliers and should be designed coherently before outsourcing or scaling edge modules.
