# Scaling market results

The simulator should not need to simulate the full addressable market.

## Method

1. Simulate a representative synthetic population.
2. Validate that portfolio statistics converge.
3. Report results per customer and per 1,000 customers.
4. Scale results by market segment assumptions.

## Caveat

Technical quantities may scale approximately linearly. Market-cleared value, mFRR prices, intraday impact and operational complexity may not scale linearly.
