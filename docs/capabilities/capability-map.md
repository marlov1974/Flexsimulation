# Capability map

Flexsimulation treats capabilities as selectable building blocks.

## System capabilities

System capabilities create value across assets, households and markets:

- customer and asset information model
- observable energy state
- 15-minute plan and baseline management
- stacked optimisation
- flexibility forecasting and risk quantification
- portfolio virtual battery management
- activation orchestration
- rebound scheduling and intraday monetisation
- delivery verification and settlement
- business case and value attribution
- cross-asset portfolio effects and dependency modelling

## Edge capabilities

Edge capabilities connect specific resources or use cases:

- EV smart charging
- EV V2X
- cabin battery
- PV forecast and curtailment
- heat pump and hot-water flexibility
- cabin heating and frost protection
- pool or premium-cabin loads
- dual-home energy sharing
- home battery optimisation

## Scenario use

Business-case scenarios should enable or disable capabilities to quantify incremental value.
